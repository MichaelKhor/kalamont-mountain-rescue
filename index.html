Kalmont Mountain Safety & Rescue

Kalmont Mountain Safety & Rescue is a volunteer-run nonprofit dedicated to keeping our community safe in the backcountry. Whether you’re hiking, skiing, or climbing, we’re here to help with expert search and rescue services, essential winter safety education, and real-time avalanche and weather updates.

Our goal is to ensure that everyone exploring Kalmont Mountain has the knowledge and support they need to stay safe.

Safety & Operations

At Kalmont, safety starts with preparation. Our volunteer-led efforts focus on prevention, awareness, and community support — not just emergency response. With trail patrols, essential gear, and up-to-date condition reports, we help backcountry users stay informed and equipped in unpredictable mountain environments.
